At a Glance

On-device processing for photos; AdMob for ads; no uploads to our servers.

Quick Summary

  • Photos you capture or pick are processed on your device. We do not upload them to our servers.
  • The App requests Camera and Media permissions so you can take a photo or select one from the gallery.
  • The App shows ads using Google AdMob; Google may collect device and usage data to serve and measure ads.
  • For privacy questions, email ayslabs.dev@gmail.com.

Data the App Accesses

Camera

When you take a photo, the App uses your device’s camera to capture an image. The image remains on your device unless you save, export, or share it.

Photos/Media

If you pick an image from your gallery, the App requests permission to read images. Only the selected image is processed locally. The App does not scan or upload your photo library.

App Usage & Diagnostics

The App itself does not collect personally identifiable analytics. Crash logs may be generated by Android or third-party SDKs (e.g., AdMob) to improve stability and advertising.

How Your Photos Are Processed

Background removal, cropping/sizing, edge smoothing, and sheet/PDF creation are performed locally using on-device processing and ML features. We never receive or store your photos.

Saved/exported items are stored locally (e.g., Downloads/PassportPhotos) on your device.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device information (such as IP address and a resettable advertising ID), approximate location, and app interaction data to serve, personalize (where allowed), and measure ads.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from apps & sites
  • Manage ad personalization

On-Device ML (Google ML Kit)

Face detection, segmentation, and alignment guidance use Google ML Kit. These features run locally on your device. No photo data is uploaded to our servers for these features.

Permissions We Request

  • CAMERA — capture photos in-app.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+) or READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (older versions) — select a photo from your gallery.
  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — display ads and check connectivity.

You can manage permissions anytime in your device settings. Some features may not work without them.

How We Use Data

  • Provide the App’s features (camera capture, gallery selection, photo processing).
  • Improve stability and reliability via system-level diagnostics.
  • Display and measure advertising (Google AdMob).

Storage & Retention

Photos: Saved or exported images and PDFs are stored locally on your device. We do not keep copies on our servers.

Ads/SDK data: Advertising partners may retain data under their own privacy policies (see Google’s links above).

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. Information is shared only with third-party SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob) to enable ads, or if required by law, to protect rights, safety, or comply with legal processes.

Security

The App processes photos entirely on your device. While we take reasonable measures appropriate to the nature of the App, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please keep your device updated and use the App responsibly.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and has no explicit age restriction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact ayslabs.dev@gmail.com so we can delete it.

International Use

The App is available worldwide. Third-party providers (e.g., Google) may process data in other countries. Please review their privacy policies for details.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Manage app permissions in your device settings (Camera, Photos/Media).
  • Control Google ad personalization at adssettings.google.com.
  • Contact us at ayslabs.dev@gmail.com with privacy questions.
